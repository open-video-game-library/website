A library for game researchers with the aim of "facilitating game research and contributing to the development of game research"

What you can do with OVGL

Finding games to use in your research

You can find and use open video games that we have created to be used in your research, or external open source games that you can edit to suit your research.

Find a game

Find tools to use in your research

You can find and use recording functions and questionnaires that are useful when conducting experiments to evaluate experiences and devices with games.

Find Tools

Deepen your knowledge of game research

You will be able to find out information that will be useful for game development and points to keep in mind when conducting game research.
