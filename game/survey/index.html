Animal Games

This is a survey of papers on the Hunter Chameleon, Escape Fish, and Sliding Penguin, the first three developed in this project. To determine the genres to be developed, we searched the ACM Digital Library and the IPSJ electronic library using the following queries.

"Title:((game AND device) OR (game AND controller) OR (game AND experience)) AND Abstract:((game AND device) OR (game AND controller) OR (game AND experience))"

From there, we conducted further screening and finally extracted 105 papers. The genre classification was based on Wolf et al.
